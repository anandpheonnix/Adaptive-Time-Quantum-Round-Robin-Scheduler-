📘 What is Adaptive RR? Standard Round Robin uses a fixed quantum for all processes.
  • Average strategy — sets quantum = mean of all burst times, balancing short and long processes.
  • Median strategy — sets quantum = median burst time, more robust to outliers.
Result: fewer context switches for short jobs, better fairness for longer ones.
